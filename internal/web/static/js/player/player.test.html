How to test (Chunks 2 + 3 + 4 + 5)
  1. Pick a station ID with multiple streams configured (HQ + LQ). Hit the in-container Play button. State should go to playing; Active stream should show HQ.
  2. Kill the HQ stream upstream. After 3 reconnect attempts inside 30s, the player should auto-degrade — Active stream flips to LQ, Escalations increments, & playback resumes. Watch for the "Reconnecting..." text flash.
  3. Bring HQ back. Within 60s the background HEAD probe should succeed & the player should step back up to HQ.
  4. Kill BOTH streams. State goes unavailable; the in-container Retry button appears (the toggle button also turns into "Retry"). Bring streams back & click Retry.
  5. Pick a specific quality from the dropdown — the player pins to it (auto-degrade off until you switch back to Auto).
  6. Check Network tab: POSTs to /api/v1/listener-events on play, stop, reconnect, degrade, upgrade, exhausted. Tick the telemetry checkbox above & rebuild to confirm they stop.
  7. Lock-screen / OS media controls (Chunk 5.1): on a phone or with the system media-key shortcut, the play/pause keys should drive the player; metadata shows the station name.
